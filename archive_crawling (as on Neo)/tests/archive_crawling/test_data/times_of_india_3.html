        Freshers join in to participate in Clean & Clear Lucknow Times Fresh Face 2012 at Shri Ramswaroop Memorial College

        By -
        TNN
        Devanshi Seth
        Updated: Mar 25, 2014, 16:55 IST
        facebooktwitterPintrest
        Prakriti Kohli
        The first season of the Clean & Clear Lucknow Times Fresh Face contest kick-started amidst great engery and enthusiasm, when the fuchchas at the Shri Ramswaroop Memorial College of Engineering and Management took to the stage with great confidence.

        The pulsating music drowned the sound of the heavy downpour outside. But even louder was the applause for the fresh faces that went all out to woo the crowd. Such was the excitement to be part of the contest that even post-grad students insisted they be allowed to participate.

        walk the talk: While some decided to sing, and others dance, most of the participants just decided to walk! Oh yes, the youngsters were big on ramp walk, and sashayed down the stage with great attitude.

        Pump it UP: Maddy, the emcee for the show, managed to get even the hesitant students up on stage, and it was from among the late bloomers that two of the six winners were announced Prakriti Kohli and Abhishek Dwivedi, the two first runners up. But the freshest face on the block was Anshima Chowdhury, who danced to the beats with such confidence that it won her the top spot along with Lov Kumar Pandey. The bright and bubbly Ankit Yadav and Riya Rastogi got the second runners up title.

        End of the article
